Eco-friendly Packing and Shipping From Greece to Portugal

More and more people are joining the eco-friendly movement, changing their habits, including how they send and receive parcels. Online businesses are also no exception. More of them are starting to offer eco-conscious items and delivery options.

And as the demand for more eco-friendly shipping from Greece to Portugal grows, courier delivery companies are discovering new ways to reduce their carbon footprint.

EcoParcel strives to offer more delivery options that are friendly to the environment and partners with door-to-door delivery companies like DPD, UPS, FedEx, and others, who are shifting from traditional delivery methods to more eco-friendly ones by:

  • Implementing solar and electricity-powered fleets and vans
  • Investing in green-energy powered infrastructure and warehouses
  • Managing label-free delivery options
  • Choosing the shortest delivery routes and combining multiple deliveries

If you want to be a part of the green movement, you can choose recyclable packing options when you deliver items to another country. Paper envelopes, boxes, and protective packaging alternatives help to reduce plastic usage drastically.

eCommerce stores can also switch to sustainable packaging and deliver the parcels through eco-friendly courier companies. Those solutions also help to boost the brand’s exposure and improve customer experience as shoppers around the globe are searching for brands that are driven by eco-consciousness.

Eco-friendly delivery from Greece to Portugal does not cost more than post office or PUDO points services but can provide faster and more reliable shipping. EcoParcel partners are trusted by millions of senders around the world and are ready to take great care of your package.

No more long waiting times, PUDO points — just a few easy steps and the door-to-door courier will be on their way to you.
