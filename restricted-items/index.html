List of Restricted Items

The items described below or any similar item cannot be sent using our services, in addition to being deemed non-compensatory.

Compensation for damage or loss will not be payable if restricted items to be sent.

It is the shipper´s responsibility to comply with current government regulations or laws applicable in each country. Shipments are subject to inspection and possible delay by customs or representatives of other government authorities.

Any items that are wet, leaking or emit an odour of any kind cannot be sent on any of our services and are deemed to be disposed due to hygiene reasons.

Suitcases have to be wrapped in the bubble wrap and placed into the double-layered box, as suitcases are not suitable for regular economy type road service.

We cannot transport (or any similar items):

Orders will lose any type of insurance if contains mentioned or similar items.

Title Prohibited Boxed
1 Aerosols 
2 Alcoholic beverages containing more than 24% but not more than 70% alcohol by volume (ABV)
3 Alcoholic Beverages
4 Arms and Ammunition
5 Batteries that are classified as dangerous goods and used batteries
6 Batteries, specifically new and used lithium batteries when <b>NOT connected</b> to an electronic device
7 Batteries - new wet, non-spillable
8 Environmental waste
9 Flammable liquids
10 Flammable solids 
11 Gases, including flammable, non-flammable, toxic and compressed gases
12 Hoverboards and electric scooters
13 Human and animal remains
14 Infectious substances and pathogens
15 Liquids over 1 litre 
16 Living creatures, bees, spiders fish fry and coral
17 Magnetized material
18 Perfumes, eau de parfum, eau de toilette
19 Pesticides 
20 Poisons, toxic liquids, solids and gases
21 Suitcases
22 Personal Items
23 Tobacco
