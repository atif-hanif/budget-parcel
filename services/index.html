ecoTrack: Branded Tracking

In order to provide a perfect shopping experience for their customers, eCommerce entrepreneurs are increasingly focusing not only on the delivery method, but also on an informative shipment tracking function.

  • Use Your Brand Logo
  • Embed Branded Tracking Widget
  • Add your Business Promotions
  • Real-time Tracking

Business customers who choose to work with Ecoparcel and to be a regular Ecoparcel partner can offer their customers a tracking function for the journey of the goods, which can be accessed by the customer by entering the unique tracking number received.
